Helping Youth Reach Their Potential

Guiding youth toward healing, growth, and a brighter future.

Blooming Bridge RTC is a Residential Treatment Center providing 24/7 supervision and therapeutic services to youth ages 6–12 in DFPS care. Our highly trained staff use evidence-based programs to support each child’s holistic development. Blooming Bridge RTC partners with DFPS and Community-Based Care Contracts. We do not accept private pay or insurance at this time.
