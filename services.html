Services

Blooming Bridge RTC provides services and treatment modalities to meet the needs of each individual resident. Click below to find out what services meet your child's unique needs

  1. Home
  2. Services

Initial and On-going Evaluations

Comprehensive evaluations to assess emotional, behavioral, medical, educational, and social needs, and support these needs safely.

Individualized Treatment Plans

An Individualized Treatment Plan that puts into place interventions that help the each child attain their individualized goals.

Therapy Sessions

Individual, group, and family therapy based on each client individual needs.

Psychiatric Care

Psychiatric care coordinated by a child and adolescent psychiatrist

Family and Legal Party Involvement

Involvement of the child's family or support system.

Crisis Prevention Intervention

Nonviolent and predictable ways to help youth with emotional and behavioral issues.

Therapeutic Recreation

Each Resident will have opportunities to participate in therapeutic recreation daily

Religious Worship Opportunities

Each Resident will have opportunities to attend church services and activities based on their religious preference.
